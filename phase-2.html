Phase 02 IP Address Design
Every subnet planned on paper before it's typed into a device. Reference
In one sentence: think of an IP range like a street of numbered houses — we're deciding how big each street is before anyone moves in.
WAN link
Why a /30 here?This link only ever has two devices on it — the ISP router and the firewall. A /30 gives exactly 2 usable addresses, no wasted space.
DeviceInterfaceIP Address
ISP Router—203.0.113.1/30

LAN network
Why a /16 for 50 people?50 people don't need 65,000 addresses today — but re-numbering a live network later is painful. A /16 leaves room to grow for years without ever revisiting this decision.
